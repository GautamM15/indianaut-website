About Us

Indianaut is an audio archive of India's startup revolution. We decode the minds of the country's most ambitious builders and investors, from founders to policy shapers to VCs, transforming the ecosystem one step at a time.

More than just a podcast, Indianaut is a community platform for the next generation of stakeholders. Whether you are an operator, investor, or aspirant, our bi-weekly conversations offer the tactical playbooks you won't find in headlines.

Hosted by Gautam Marwah, the platform sits at the intersection of curiosity and product development principles, asking the questions that matter to those building the future.
